Welcome! Get to know me:

Growing up, my mother toiled on a Vietnamese rice field traversing rugged terrain barefoot. My success and pursuit are not in spite of my background, but because of it. This instilled in me a profound appreciation for resilience and resourcefulness. These qualities have propelled me towards success in my career as a Biochemist, where I developed life-changing medications for patients who lacked access to quality healthcare. Through this work, I learned the value of tenacity in the face of failure, a trait I also apply as a Software Engineer. My desire to broaden my impact and reach more individuals led me to pursue a career in software engineering, where I can develop impactful applications that can make a tangible difference in people's lives. My background has imbued me with a unique perspective, one that drives me to tackle challenges with empathy and determination - and I am eager to leverage my skills and knowledge towards this endeavor. So let's see what we can build together!
